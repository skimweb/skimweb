WEB DEVELOPMENT TRENDS IN 2023

As every trend keeps changing, so do the web development trends. In 2023, although many of the trends are carried over from the previous year,there are a few additions due to technological advancements.

Today is the age of experience, comfort, and convenience. Every customer wants everything to be right where they are.

As a result, e-commerce evolved to provide everything at one's doorstep. Even in the virtual world, experience, comfort and convenience rule. This has paved the way for the incorporation of voice search, artificial intelligence, virtual reality, machine learning, and other technologies into web development. Not only that, but an increasing emphasis is also being placed on the responsiveness and acessibility of websites in order to provide the best user experience possible.

As it takes shape, web development is scaling new heights with these technologies, which we'll go over one by one in ourr web development in 2023 blog.

TYPES OF SITES
Static or interactive websites

There are two main types of sites-interactive and static:
-Interactive sites form part of the web 2.0 community of sites. They allow for interactivity between their owners and visitors or users. Put simply, in this type of website you can interact with it.
-Static sites capture or serve information. However, they o not allow interactivity with the users or audience directly.
The Web has many different types of websites, each specializing in a particular type of use or content. We classify them as:
-Affiliate-a site with few pages whose purpose is to sell a third party's good or service. The owner of the website subsequently recieves a commission on sales.
-Archive sites are used to preserve valuable electronic content. In fact, many face the threat of extinction.
-Attack sites have malicious intent. In other words, people specifically design them to attack visitors' computers, tablets, or smartphones.
-Blogs(Web Logs) are typically used to post online diaries which often include discussion forums. Owners of blogs are called 'bloggers'.
-Crowdfunding sites are platforms to raise finance or sponsorship for projects. People can pre-purchase goods. They can also make donation.

There is one thing all the most successful websites have in common. They have a very simple look and design. Therefore, when you develop a website, above all,follow the KISS principle.KISS stands for Keep It Simple, Stupid! Complicated websites fail!
